House-Museum of Orbel brothers

In 1972, in order to perpetuate the memory of the Orbeli brothers, the house where academician Levon Orbeli was born was turned into a house-museum by Resolution No. 43 of the Council of Ministers of the ASSR dated July 21.
It is included in the list of immovable monuments of history and culture of Tsaghkadzor.
Story
The house-museum of the Orbel brothers was opened on July 6, 1982, as part of the 100th anniversary celebrations of academician Levon Orbel. The purpose of the house-museum is to preserve and pass on to generations the spiritual values ??and physical evidences associated with the name of the Orbel brothers. The museum is under the authority of the RA Ministry of Culture.
