Byurakan Observatory 

Observatory in Byurakan village of Armenia, on the slope of Mount Aragats. It belongs to the Academy of Sciences of Armenia and is managed through it. It is considered one of the most important observatories in Eastern Europe and the Middle East.
Story
It was founded in 1946 by the efforts of academician Viktor Hambardzumyan, he was the first director of the observatory. The architect is Samvel Safaryan.
Hayk Badalyan, Beniamin Margaryan, Ruben Sahakyan, Grigor Gurzadyan, Ludvig Mirzoyan, Nina Ivanova, Vagharshak Sanamyan, Marat Arakelyan, Edward Khachikyan and Carlos Grigoryan were among the first astronomers of the Byurakan Observatory.
