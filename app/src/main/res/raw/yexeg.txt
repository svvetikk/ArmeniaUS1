Yeghegnadzor Geology Museum

Yeghegnadzor Geology Museum was founded in 1968. The museum is one of the most important cultural and educational centers of both Yeghegnadzor and Vayots Dzor marz, as well as a place of tourist entertainment. The museum contributes to the popularization and dissemination of national culture and historical heritage with its activities. Scientific research related to culture and history and many cultural events and educational programs are regularly held. Since 2017, the museum has been cooperating with the Smithsonian Institution within the framework of the "My Armenia" program. It is included in the list of immovable monuments of history and culture of Yeghegnadzor.
