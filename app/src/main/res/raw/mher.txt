Museum of Mher Mkrtchyan

The museum of Mher Mkrtchyan, People's Artist of the USSR, a great comedian of cinema and theater, was founded in 2004, the opening took place on December 20, 2006, in a building representing historical and architectural value built at the end of the 19th century.
Events
On the way to the development of museum works, the tourist guide "Museums of Public History" was created. Thematic matinees, presentations, readings of abstracts are held in the museum, with the participation of various educational institutions and public organizations.
