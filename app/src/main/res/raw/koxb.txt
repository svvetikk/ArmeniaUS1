Koghb History Museum

Koghb History Museum, a museum dedicated to history, ethnography in the village of Koghb, Tavush Marz, Republic of Armenia.
As a rural museum, its format is unique in the entire Tavush region. It was founded in 1970 by Suren Abovyan.
In the museum, the history, lifestyle and life of Koghb and surrounding areas are presented as much as possible. In total, the museum has about 1700 exhibits. The collection of the museum includes 3,000-3,700-year-old pottery and bronze items, historical coins, and photographs of different periods and themes. The oldest exhibit of the museum is located in Koghb and BC. It is a metal sword found in a tomb dating back to the 9th century.
